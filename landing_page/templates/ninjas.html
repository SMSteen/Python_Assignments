Interesting Facts About Ninjas

Ninjas, also known as shinobi, appeared on the scene during the Sengoku period of the 15th century. They were a group of specially trained spies and mercenaries.

Ninjas were known for their espionage skills, gathering information on enemy terrain, building specifications, passwords and communications.

Ninjas did not work alone; they relied heavily on their team. For example, to scale a wall, one ninja must rely on the team to provide a platform to assist the individual in reaching greater heights.

Ninjas used a large variety of tools to get the job done.

Useless Trivia

  • Ninjas consumed a vegetarian diet in order to avoid body odor.
  • Ninjas often used simple gardening tools, such as sickles, as weapons. In the unlikely event they were discovered, they could claim the tools were simply tools, not weapons.
  • Women were ninjas too. Mochizuki Chiyome (16th century) created a school for girls, which taught skills required of geisha, as well as espionage skills.
